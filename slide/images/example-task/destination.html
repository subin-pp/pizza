TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal is an iconic mausoleum located in Agra, India, renowned for its stunning white marble architecture and intricate details. Commissioned in 1632 by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is a masterpiece of Mughal architecture and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

EIFFEL TOWER

The Eiffel Tower, located in Paris, France, is a wrought-iron lattice tower completed in 1889 for the Exposition Universelle.

COLOSSEUM

The Colosseum, located in Rome, Italy, is an iconic symbol of ancient Rome. Completed in AD 80, this grand amphitheater was used for gladiatorial contests, public spectacles, and theatrical performances. With a seating capacity of up to 80,000 spectators, the Colosseum is renowned for its impressive architecture and engineering.
